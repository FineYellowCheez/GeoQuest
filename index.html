📖 How to Play

🗺️ The Map

Your real GPS location is shown as a blue dot. Move around in real life to explore — items and Dice Stations are scattered across America.

📦 Collecting Items

Dumpsters and pawn shops are scattered randomly all across the country — not tied to any particular city. You must be within 1 km of something to collect it (a Reach Ticket extends this to 5 km for 5 minutes, or a Super Reach Ticket extends it to 50 km for one single use).

🎲 Dice Stations

Dice Stations are scattered nationwide, same as items. Roll for gold and XP — each station has a 30-minute cooldown after use. Luck Tickets improve your odds. You can also buy a Portable Dice Station and place your own permanent station anywhere.

⚔ Seasons & Teams

Join one of 2-6 teams (set with the slider on the menu screen). Each team has its own arm on the X-shaped board with 10 sections that must be completed in order:

1) Collect Fabric. 2) Collect Scrap Metal x2. 3) Collect your team's colored Dye (must match your team's color!). 4) Collect Scissors. 5) Collect Wood. 6) Collect a Pole. 7) Deposit 500 🪙 Gold into the Team Vault. 8) Use 5 Dice Stations. 9) Construct your flag at a Workbench. 10) Plant your flag in America — first team there wins the Season!

🏦 Team Vaults

Deposit gold and items into your team's vault from the Seasons tab. Deposits are permanent — nothing can ever be withdrawn.

🚩 Crafting Your Flag

Flags can't be bought — they must be crafted. Find Fabric, Scrap Metal, Scissors, Wood, a Pole, and your team's colored Dye in dumpsters 🗑️ and pawn shops 🏪 scattered across the map. Once you have everything, visit a Workbench 🛠️ (also scattered nationwide) to construct your flag. Then place it from your Bag at your current location to win the Season.

🎟️ Tickets

Buy tickets in the Shop for temporary boosts: Luck, XP Boost, Reach, Super Reach, Double Drop, Cooldown Skip, Sprint, Vault Insurance, and Scanner. You can also buy a Portable Dice Station to place your own permanent station anywhere on the map.

🤖 Single Player & Bots

In Single Player, every team you didn't choose is played by a bot. Each bot wanders around the country on land (they can't enter water) and periodically gathers materials, deposits into their vault, crafts their flag, and plants it — so your team has real competition even when playing alone.

🤝 Trading

Get within range of a bot (tap its marker, or use the Players & Bots list in the Explore tab) and tap Trade. Offer gold or an item from your Bag and request something from their stash — but bots judge whether a deal is fair to them, so they won't always say yes, and you don't have to accept any deal either.
