Choose Your Team

You'll play as one team. The other 3 teams will be controlled by bots — each spawns somewhere random in America and moves around on their own.

📖 How to Play

🗺️ The Map

Your real GPS location is shown as a blue dot. Move around in real life to explore — items, Dice Stations, and special cities are scattered across America.

📦 Collecting Items

Dumpsters and pawn shops are scattered randomly all across the country — not tied to any particular city. You must be within 1 km of something to collect it (a Reach Ticket extends this to 5 km for 5 minutes, or a Super Reach Ticket extends it to 50 km for one single use).

🎲 Dice Stations

Dice Stations are scattered nationwide, same as items. Roll for gold and XP — each station has a 30-minute cooldown after use. Luck Tickets improve your odds. You can also buy a Portable Dice Station and place your own permanent station anywhere.

⭐ Special Cities

Cities like Las Vegas, NYC, and others have a unique Special Action with its own 30-minute cooldown and bigger rewards.

⚔ Seasons & Teams

Join one of 4 teams. Each team has its own arm on the X-shaped board with 10 sections that must be completed in order. Sections require things like vault deposits, Dice Station use, item collecting, and visiting special cities. The 10th and final section requires planting your team's flag anywhere in America — first team there wins the Season! Flags can only be bought once your team reaches this final section.

🏦 Team Vaults

Deposit gold and items into your team's vault from the Seasons tab. Deposits are permanent — nothing can ever be withdrawn.

🚩 Flags

Once you're on a team, buy Flags in the Shop (🪙500 each) and place them from your Bag at your current location. They show team pride and count toward your team's final quest.

🎟️ Tickets

Buy tickets in the Shop for temporary boosts: Luck, XP Boost, Reach, Super Reach, Double Drop, Cooldown Skip, Sprint, Vault Insurance, and Scanner. You can also buy a Portable Dice Station to place your own permanent station anywhere on the map.

🤖 Single Player & Bots

In Single Player, the 3 teams you didn't choose are played by bots. Each bot wanders around the country on land (they can't enter water) and periodically deposits into their vault and places flags — so your team has real competition even when playing alone.
